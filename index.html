I love reading

I love reading—it’s my favourite way to unwind and switch off. I enjoy all genres, but sci-fi and detective stories are my absolute favourites, especially anything with a good mystery or a futuristic twist. Lately, I’ve been reading On the Calculation of Volume by Solvej Balle On the Calculation of Volume, which was shortlisted for the International Booker Prize. I also really enjoyed the audiobook version. I’m excited for book five—there will be seven in total, with the final one due in November 2027. I am still deciding what I will be reading next!
